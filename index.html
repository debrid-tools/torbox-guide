01 Don't throw away free premium days

TorBox hands out +7 extra days of premium for every month you buy when you apply a referral code before your first purchase. That bonus is real, recurring time added on top of what you paid for — and it only ever happens once, on that very first checkout.

The catch? Most people grab a random referral code from a forum or a stranger and never check whether it actually works. A referral code only grants you bonus days if the person who owns it is on a paid plan at the moment you buy. If they're on the free plan, their premium has expired, or they only ever had the 24-hour free trial, you get nothing.

A stranger's code is therefore a gamble. You hand over your one-time referral slot, and if their account isn't eligible you've quietly thrown away weeks of premium. TorBox support will not manually add a missed bonus or swap the code afterward — the referral is locked to that first purchase.

That is the whole reason this guide exists. Below is a referral code whose account is checked automatically every day through the official TorBox API, with the result shown right on this page. You can see — before you buy — that the referrer is on an active paid plan, so your bonus is actually going to land.

Already have a TorBox account with a previous plan? The referral bonus is only awarded on the first purchase of a new account. If you've already bought premium on your current account, it may be worth creating a fresh account to qualify for the bonus — the extra days you'll gain far outweigh starting over.

06 Must-know referral conditions

Straight from TorBox's referral rules. Read these once so you do not lose your bonus.

  • To receive premium time for referring someone, you must have a paid TorBox plan. If you are on the free plan and someone uses your code, you receive nothing.
  • The same logic applies if you're trying to use someone's referral code: if the referrer does not have a paid plan, you will not receive any referral bonus. The 24-hour free trial does not count toward referrals and will not provide a bonus.
  • Referrals are only given on the first purchase. Subsequent purchases will not receive the referral bonus.
  • Both the referrer and the purchaser receive 7 days per month purchased. A yearly subscription multiplies the bonus by 12 → 84 days. 6 months → 42 days, 3 months → 21 days.
  • Support will not manually adjust accounts for referrals for any reason, including reinstating a lost bonus.
  • Support will not add referral codes to accounts. You must add your referral code to the bottom of the subscription page.
  • Referral bonuses and plan discounts are stackable — you can benefit from both at the same time.
  • Manual payments such as gift cards or redeemed vouchers do not qualify for referral time and do not count as a "first purchase". If your current premium time is purely from redeemed vouchers, you are not eligible to benefit from referring others — but you are still eligible for a referral bonus if you purchase time for your own account.

07 Quick FAQ

Can I use the referral code on a second purchase?

No. Referral bonuses are only applied on your first purchase. Make sure to add the code before you check out the first time.

What if the referrer's plan expires?

If the referrer no longer has a paid plan at the time of your purchase, you will not receive the bonus. That is exactly why this guide checks the referrer's plan daily — check the badge above before you buy.

Do gift cards or vouchers count as a first purchase?

No. Manual payments like gift cards and redeemed vouchers do not qualify for referral time and do not count as a "first purchase".

Can I stack a referral bonus with a plan discount?

Yes. Referral bonuses and plan discounts are stackable, so you can benefit from both at the same time.
